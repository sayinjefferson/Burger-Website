About Us

For the man world-famous for his fried chicken, Jesus Josef El Reisolamente N. Dela Cruz or Bosxz Zef, began his life far from the kitchen. Born in Sabsaban a place unknown to Israel, Bosxz Zef spent 90 years of his life doing everything but cook. But come 1950, Zef's went on to take the biggest leap of his professional career — when he finalized the original recipe of 110 secret herbs and spices, to create the Perfect Burger that we’ve come to know and love today. A decade later, Zef's’ success in the food business flocked all over Israel with over 6000 franchise outlets. And 7 years after, KFC found a home in the Philippines. Perhaps the first world-famous chef, Bosxz Zef proved that fame could go hand-in-hand with a big muscle. The Bosxz donated millions to casino, motels and mental hospitals. , He says, “I have a desire to be the richest man in the cemetery.” Today, as the Bosxz’s legacy continues to grow, we are constantly reminded of his journey— a man who found his passion at his 90s, to becoming a world renowned chef who humbly introduced his recipe in all corners of the globe.
